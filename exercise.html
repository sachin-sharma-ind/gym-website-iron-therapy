Muscle Group Exercises

Chest Exercises

Bench Press

The bench press is a fundamental strength-training exercise that targets the pectoral muscles, triceps, and shoulders.

Push-Ups

Push-ups are a bodyweight exercise that work the chest, triceps, and shoulders. They are great for building upper body strength.

Incline Dumbbell Press

The incline dumbbell press targets the upper part of the chest and helps in building a well-rounded chest.

Cable Fly

Cable flies focus on the inner part of the chest and are great for adding definition and size to the pectoral muscles.

Chest Dips

Chest dips are an excellent compound exercise that targets the lower chest and triceps, promoting overall upper body strength.

Back Exercises

Pull-Ups

Pull-ups are a challenging bodyweight exercise that primarily targets the lats, along with the biceps and upper back.

Deadlifts

Deadlifts are a full-body exercise that strengthens the back, glutes, hamstrings, and core.

Barbell Row

The barbell row is a compound movement that targets the middle back, lats, and biceps.

Lat Pulldowns

Lat pulldowns are a great exercise for building the width of the back and developing the lat muscles.

Single-Arm Dumbbell Row

The single-arm dumbbell row targets the lats, traps, and rhomboids, providing unilateral strength development.

Biceps Exercises

Barbell Curl

Barbell curls are a fundamental exercise for building bicep size and strength.

Dumbbell Curl

Dumbbell curls allow for a greater range of motion and can help correct muscle imbalances.

Hammer Curl

Hammer curls target the brachialis muscle, which can help increase overall arm size and strength.

Concentration Curl

Concentration curls isolate the biceps for maximum muscle contraction and development.

Cable Curl

Cable curls provide constant tension on the biceps, which can lead to greater muscle growth.

Triceps Exercises

Tricep Dips

Tricep dips are an effective bodyweight exercise that primarily targets the triceps and chest.

Overhead Tricep Extension

The overhead tricep extension isolates the triceps for maximum contraction and growth.

Tricep Pushdown

Tricep pushdowns are a cable exercise that target all three heads of the triceps.

Close-Grip Bench Press

The close-grip bench press focuses on the triceps while also engaging the chest and shoulders.

Skull Crushers

Skull crushers are a powerful tricep exercise that involve lowering a weight towards the forehead and extending the arms.

Leg Exercises

Squats

Squats are a foundational leg exercise that targets the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and core.

Leg Press

The leg press is a machine exercise that emphasizes the quads, hamstrings, and glutes.

Lunges

Lunges are a unilateral exercise that target the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and improve balance.

Leg Curl

Leg curls isolate the hamstrings, promoting muscle growth and strength in the back of the legs.

Calf Raises

Calf raises target the calf muscles, enhancing lower leg strength and endurance.

Abs Exercises

Crunches

Crunches are a classic abs exercise that focus on the upper abdominal muscles.

Plank

The plank is an isometric exercise that engages the entire core, including the abs, back, and shoulders.

Russian Twists

Russian twists target the obliques and help in building a strong and defined core.

Leg Raises

Leg raises are a lower abs exercise that also engage the hip flexors and improve core stability.

Bicycle Crunches

Bicycle crunches are a dynamic exercise that targets the entire abdominal region, including the obliques.
